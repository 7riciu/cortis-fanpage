CORTIS (코르티스) is a South Korean boy group formed by BigHit Music, known for launching global acts like BTS and TOMORROW X TOGETHER. The group debuted on August 18, 2025, with the digital single "What You Want," marking BigHit's first boy group debut in six years The name CORTIS is derived from the phrase "Color Outside the Lines", symbolizing the group's commitment to creative freedom and breaking conventional norms
