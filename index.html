CORTIS (코르티스) is a South Korean boy group formed by BigHit Music. They debuted on August 18, 2025, with the digital single "What You Want," the lead track from their first extended play (EP), Color Outside the Lines.

The name is derived from six letters taken from the phrase "Color Outside the Lines," symbolizing the group's commitment to thinking freely and unconstrained by societal norms.
